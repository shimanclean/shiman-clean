About us

Shiman Clean — a professional cleaning company in Lviv that brings cleanliness, comfort and impeccable service. Most popular questions

Do you provide services outside the city?

Yes, we provide services outside the city — details and travel cost are calculated individually depending on the distance.

How can I pay?

You can pay for our cleaning services in any convenient way: cash, bank card or bank transfer.

Do I need to be at home during cleaning?

Your presence during cleaning is not required — just agree on details in advance and provide access to the premises.

Can I order cleaning only for certain parts of the premises?

Yes, of course. You can order cleaning only for specific areas (kitchen, bathroom) or even cleaning of specific appliances like oven or refrigerator.

What does the cleaning price depend on?

The price depends on:
– Size of apartment/house
– Level of contamination
– Type of service ordered

Is window cleaning included in the price?

No, it is not included. Cleaning and window washing are performed by different cleaners, but we can do them on the same day.

If a cleaner damages property, who compensates?

Our staff treats clients’ property carefully. We have not had such cases, but if it happens, we guarantee compensation for damages caused by our cleaners.
