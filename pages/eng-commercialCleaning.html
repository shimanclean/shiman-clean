Commercial cleaning

Commercial cleaning ensures cleanliness and order in your business space. We carefully look after offices, shops and other commercial properties premises A clean environment creates a professional image and comfort for employees and customers.

Our Commercial Cleaning Process

1

Object Evaluation

We analyze the area and specifics of the premises, take into account the number of workers zones and attendance to make an optimal plan.

2

Selection of means

We use professional and safe means for office furniture, equipment, shopping areas and sanitary zones.

3

Basic cleaning

We perform system cleaning: floor, furniture, work tables, appliances, kitchen and bathrooms — we bring everything to perfect condition.

4

Details and trifles

We clean door handles, switches, plinths, ventilation grills and other important elements that form a sense of cleanliness.

5

Final control

We check the quality of the work done, collect the garbage and leave it space tidy, safe and ready for work.
