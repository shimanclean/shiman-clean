Standard cleaning

Standard cleaning helps keep your home tidy. We thoroughly clean the main areas and surfaces. We take care of everyone detail to make you feel comfortable. A clean environment creates peace and freshness every day.

What is included in standard cleaning:

  • Kitchen

    • Degreasing of countertops and facades
    • Washing the stove and appliances outside
    • Cleaning and polishing of sink and faucet
    • Disinfection of the place for garbage, removal of the package

  • Bathroom

    • Washing the toilet, bathtub/shower
    • Cleaning the sink and mirrors
    • Polishing of tiles and chrome parts
    • Disinfection and garbage removal

  • Rooms

    • Removing dust from furniture and surfaces
    • Wiping windowsills, batteries, doors, switches
    • Mirror and glass washing
    • Cob removal

  • Corridor

    • Carpet vacuuming
    • Washing the floor and plinths
    • Wiping doors, switches, sockets

Our standard cleaning process

1

Assessment of the premises

We inspect the premises, determine the area and complexity of the work, to create an optimal cleaning plan.

2

Selection of means

We select professional and safe products for floors, furniture, kitchen surfaces and plumbing.

3

Basic cleaning

We perform basic cleaning: floor, furniture, appliances, kitchen and the bathroom are cleaned thoroughly and systematically.

4

Details and corners

We pay attention to small things - doors, handles, switches, plinths and hard-to-reach places under furniture.

5

Final control

We make a final check, collect garbage and leave space clean, tidy and comfortable.
